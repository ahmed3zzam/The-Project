
# Online Job System
Online Job Recruitment refers to the process of using internet-based platforms and tools to attract, assess, and hire individuals for employment opportunities. This approach leverages digital technologies to streamline the recruitment process, making it more efficient, accessible, and cost-effective for both employers and job seekers.
